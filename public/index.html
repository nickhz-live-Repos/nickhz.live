About

I'm Nick, and this is a space of my own creation.

The Web can be whatever you want it to be, and I'll make this into my collection of examples.

I am an American lifelong learner, a mechanical engineering graduate from the Georgia Institute of Technology, who has worn many hats & explored many interests. As the tagline says, I'm building this from scratch. It may look like nothing else and provide things that don't exist elsewhere, as this is a product of my own mind.

I've begun this while employed full-time, making this with some of my free time. The desire to learn and the desire to create drive me. It's the love of the craft. Of many crafts. I'm lucky enough to have passion for it, and this site will be a growing proof of that. My capabilities will grow a little more with each creation, and each new creation may be a little better done than the last.

There is still much to learn. Much to explore. And much to create.
